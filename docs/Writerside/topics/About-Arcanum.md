# About Arcanum

**Arcanum** is an unofficial, community-developed modding tool designed for Europa Universalis V, with the primary goal of
making the modding process more accessible, efficient and intuitive for new and experienced creators alike.

By providing a visual, user-friendly interface, **Arcanum** eliminates the need for manual searching and editing of script
files. This allows you to modify game objects with just a few clicks, handling all the heavy, repetitive work so you can
focus on your creative vision. Whether you are making a minor adjustment or undertaking a major overhaul, **Arcanum** aims
to streamline your workflow and help you bring your ideas to life.
